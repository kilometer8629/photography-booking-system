Santa Pet Photography

Capture the holiday magic with Santa Pet Photography, where your beloved pets become the stars of a festive photo session! Families come in all shapes and sizes, including pets. Whether it’s a dog, cat, lizard or bird, our team are ready to capture the best festive photo of your furry (or not so furry!) friends.

Our team of professional photographers are not just experts in capturing stunning images; they are also skilled pet whisperers trained to handle and care for your beloved animals with the utmost attention and affection. We know that every animal is different, so we approach each session with patience, kindness and a gentle touch.

From Fluffy to Feathered

Whether your pet has fur, feathers or scales, we’ve had the pleasure of capturing their unique personalities in our festive photo sessions. You can trust us to create magical moments for your beloved pets—but be sure to check with your local shopping centre about their animal policy for Santa sessions before booking.

Pet Photo FAQs

Can I bring multiple pets?

Yes! We love seeing multiple pets in one session. Please let us know the number and type of pets when you book so we can prepare accordingly.

What should my pet wear?

Feel free to dress your pet in festive accessories or keep it simple. We provide a few props on set, but you’re welcome to bring your own.

Are exotic pets welcome?

We’ve photographed many animals, from snakes and lizards to exotic birds. However, please check your local centre’s policy before booking.
