Our Santas are exceptionally professional and well trained to create a memorable experience for you and your family. With their friendly and welcoming demeanour they skilfully pose with children and offer creative photo ideas, ensuring every moment is magical and uniquely yours.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book to have my photos taken with Santa?

Yes. Santa photo sessions are limited, so we recommend making a booking as soon as possible.

When do bookings open?

Santa photography bookings generally open two weeks prior to Santa’s arrival.

I downloaded my photos but can’t find them.

Your downloaded photos will appear in your “Downloads” folder on desktop or the “Files” app on mobile devices.
