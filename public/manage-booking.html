Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes! You can cancel or reschedule your booking anytime. Refunds are subject to our cancellation policy based on how close to your session date you cancel.

How do I reschedule my booking?

Click "Reschedule Booking" above and let us know your preferred new date and time. We'll confirm availability and send you confirmation details.

What's your cancellation policy?

See the policy box above for details. Generally: 21+ days = full refund, 8-20 days = 50%, less than 7 days = non-refundable but reschedule available.

Will I be refunded to the same card?

Yes, refunds are processed to your original payment method and typically appear within 5-7 business days.

Need more help?

Contact us at (123) 456-7890 or bookings@amanphotography.com and we'll be happy to assist!
