What is Sensitive Santa?

Sensitive Santa offers an inclusive and accessible photo experience designed especially for children with disability. This special encounter provides a calm and accommodating environment where families can enjoy a personalised visit tailored to the needs and comfort of each child. With thoughtful adaptations, such as sensory‑friendly settings and longer time slots, Sensitive Santa ensures every child can create joyful memories and experience the magic of the holiday season in a way that suits them best.

Benefits of a Sensitive Session

Inclusive Experience

Tailored to accommodate various disabilities, ensuring that every child can enjoy the holiday magic.

Sensory‑Friendly

Designed to minimise overstimulation with quieter settings, soft lighting and gentle interactions.

Longer Session

Allows for a relaxed, unhurried visit, giving each child ample time to feel comfortable and enjoy the experience.

Outside Hours

Sessions are scheduled outside regular trading hours to minimise distractions, noise and crowds.

Personal Attention

Santa and staff are trained to understand and address the unique needs of each child, creating a comfortable visit.

Positive Interactions

Offers an opportunity for children to engage with Santa in a supportive way, fostering positive social experiences.

Questions?

What makes Sensitive Santa unique?

Sessions are designed for children with disability, offering sensory‑friendly settings, longer time slots and personalised attention.

Can I bring siblings to a Sensitive Santa session?

Yes! We welcome the whole family. Please let us know ahead of time so that we can ensure a relaxed and accommodating environment.

How do I prepare my child for the session?

We suggest bringing any comfort items your child may need. Feel free to contact us in advance to discuss specific requirements.
